Round-the-Clock Emergency Plumbing in Nottingham

Plumbing emergencies never wait for a convenient moment. A burst pipe can flood your home in minutes, a blocked sewer can render your property uninhabitable, and a gas leak poses immediate danger. When these situations arise in Nottingham, our emergency plumbing team is ready to respond immediately, any time of day or night.

Our dedicated emergency response unit operates independently from our scheduled appointments, ensuring that urgent calls receive priority attention. We maintain fully-stocked vehicles positioned across Nottingham, enabling us to reach most locations within 45-60 minutes of your call.

Emergency Situations We Handle

  • Burst Pipes: Water escaping from burst pipes causes extensive damage rapidly. Our emergency team locates and repairs bursts quickly, minimising water damage to your property.
  • Major Leaks: Significant leaks from tanks, cylinders, or hidden pipes require urgent attention before they cause structural damage or dangerous conditions.
  • Blocked Drains: When sewage backs up into your property or external drains overflow, immediate professional intervention prevents health hazards and property damage.
  • Boiler Failures: Complete heating loss during cold weather can be dangerous, particularly for vulnerable residents. Our Gas Safe engineers respond to urgent boiler failures.
  • Gas Leaks: If you smell gas, evacuate immediately and call the gas emergency line first, then contact us for subsequent repair work.
  • No Water Supply: Complete loss of water indicates serious problems requiring professional diagnosis and repair.

What Happens When You Call

Our emergency line connects you directly with an experienced operator who assesses your situation and dispatches the nearest available plumber. While help is on the way, we provide guidance on immediate steps you can take—such as locating your stopcock or turning off your boiler—to prevent further damage.

Our emergency plumbers arrive with comprehensive equipment and common spare parts, resolving most situations during the initial visit. For complex repairs requiring specialist parts, we make your system safe and return at the earliest opportunity to complete permanent repairs.

Coverage Across Nottinghamshire

Our emergency service extends throughout Nottingham city and across the wider county. Whether you're in West Bridgford, Arnold, Beeston, or the city centre, help is never far away.

Emergency Response Times

We aim to reach most Nottingham locations within one hour of your call. Response times may vary during exceptionally busy periods or adverse weather conditions, but we always keep you informed with realistic arrival estimates.

Fair Emergency Pricing

Emergency callouts are charged at competitive rates that reflect the unsociable hours and rapid response provided. We confirm pricing before starting work, so you're never surprised by the bill. Our rates remain consistent regardless of time or day—we don't inflate prices during nights, weekends, or holidays.
