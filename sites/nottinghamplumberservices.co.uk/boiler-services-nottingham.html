Complete Boiler Services in Nottingham

Your boiler works hard throughout the year, providing central heating during winter and hot water year-round. Like any complex appliance, it requires professional care to operate safely and efficiently. Our Gas Safe registered engineers deliver comprehensive boiler services across Nottingham, from emergency repairs to planned installations.

Boiler Repairs

When your boiler develops a fault, you need swift, effective repairs to restore heating and hot water. Our engineers diagnose problems accurately using their extensive experience and manufacturer training. Common issues we repair include:

  • No heating or hot water output
  • Error codes and warning lights
  • Strange noises during operation
  • Pressure fluctuations
  • Pilot light failures
  • Leaking or dripping from the boiler unit
  • Radiators not heating properly

We carry common spare parts for major manufacturers, enabling same-day repairs for many faults. For specialist components, we source parts quickly and return to complete repairs at the earliest opportunity.

Annual Boiler Servicing

Regular servicing maintains boiler efficiency and helps prevent breakdowns. During a service, our engineer inspects all components, cleans essential parts, checks gas pressure and flue operation, and verifies safe operation. Annual servicing is recommended by all manufacturers and may be required to maintain warranty coverage.

Benefits of regular servicing include improved energy efficiency, extended boiler lifespan, early identification of developing problems, and maintained manufacturer warranty. We send service reminders to help you stay on schedule.

New Boiler Installation

If your boiler is beyond economical repair or you want to upgrade to a more efficient model, we install new boilers from leading manufacturers. Our installation service includes assessment of your heating needs, recommendation of suitable boiler types and sizes, removal and disposal of your old boiler, professional installation to manufacturer specifications, system commissioning and testing, and registration with Gas Safe and the manufacturer.

Brands We Service

Our engineers are trained to work on all major boiler brands including Worcester Bosch, Vaillant, Baxi, Ideal, Glow-worm, Potterton, Viessmann, and many others. Whether you have a modern combi boiler or a traditional system boiler, we have the expertise to service and repair it.

Why Gas Safe Registration Matters

It's illegal for anyone to work on gas appliances without Gas Safe registration. This registration ensures engineers have demonstrated competence through rigorous assessment. All our gas engineers are registered, and we can provide registration numbers for verification on the official Gas Safe Register.

Coverage Throughout Nottinghamshire

Our boiler services extend across Nottingham and surrounding locations including West Bridgford, Beeston, Arnold, and throughout Nottinghamshire.
