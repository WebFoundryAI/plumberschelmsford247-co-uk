Expert Drain Services in Nottingham

Blocked drains disrupt daily routines and can quickly escalate into serious problems. From slow-draining sinks to complete sewage backups, drainage issues demand professional attention. Our specialist drainage team serves Nottingham with advanced equipment and extensive experience, clearing blockages efficiently and identifying underlying problems that could cause future issues.

Types of Blockages We Clear

  • Blocked Toilets: Whether caused by excessive paper, foreign objects, or problems in the main line, we clear toilet blockages quickly and hygienically.
  • Kitchen Drain Blockages: Grease, food particles, and soap accumulate over time, eventually restricting or blocking kitchen drains completely.
  • Bathroom Drain Blockages: Hair, soap residue, and cosmetics combine to form stubborn blockages in showers, baths, and basins.
  • External Drain Blockages: Leaves, debris, root intrusion, and ground settlement affect external drains and gullies.
  • Main Sewer Blockages: Problems with your main sewer line affect all drainage and require prompt professional intervention.

Our Drainage Equipment

High-Pressure Water Jetting: Our professional jetting equipment delivers powerful water jets that cut through grease, roots, and debris. This method is highly effective while remaining safe for modern pipework.

CCTV Drain Surveys: When the cause of problems isn't obvious, we deploy camera systems to inspect drains internally. This reveals blockage locations, pipe damage, root intrusion, and other issues without excavation.

Mechanical Rodding: For certain blockages, traditional rodding remains effective. We carry various tools for different drain sizes and blockage types.

Emergency Drain Services

Some drainage situations can't wait. When sewage is backing up into your property or drains are overflowing externally, our emergency team responds quickly to resolve the immediate problem and prevent further damage.

Preventive Drain Maintenance

Regular drain cleaning prevents blockages before they occur. We offer scheduled maintenance for both residential and commercial properties, keeping drains flowing freely and identifying developing problems early.
