Bathroom Installation Nottingham

Complete bathroom fitting service from design to finishing touches

Transform Your Bathroom

A well-designed bathroom enhances daily life and adds value to your property. Whether you're updating a tired suite, converting a spare room, or creating an en-suite, our bathroom installation service delivers stunning results. We manage every aspect of the project, ensuring seamless coordination between different trades and consistent quality throughout.

Our Installation Process

Consultation and Design: Every project begins with understanding your vision. We discuss your requirements, preferences, and budget, then help you explore options for layout, fixtures, and finishes. Our experience with Nottingham properties means we can advise on practical considerations specific to your home.

Planning and Preparation: Once designs are agreed, we create a detailed project plan covering all work phases. We coordinate deliveries, schedule trades, and ensure minimal disruption to your household. For larger projects, we can arrange temporary facilities if needed.

Removal and Preparation: Our team carefully removes existing fixtures, tiles, and fittings. We dispose of waste responsibly and prepare surfaces for new installations, addressing any underlying issues discovered during strip-out.

Plumbing and Installation: All plumbing work is completed to the highest standards, with proper connections for baths, showers, toilets, basins, and any additional features. We ensure adequate water pressure, proper drainage, and consider future maintenance access.

Finishing Touches: Tiling, flooring, painting, and final fixtures complete the transformation. We pay careful attention to details like grouting, silicone sealing, and hardware fitting that make the difference between adequate and exceptional.

Bathroom Features We Install

  • Walk-in showers and wet rooms

  • Toilets including wall-hung models

Quality Guaranteed

All bathroom installations come with our workmanship guarantee. We use quality materials from trusted suppliers and ensure all plumbing connections are properly tested before handover. If any issues arise with our work within the guarantee period, we return promptly to resolve them.
