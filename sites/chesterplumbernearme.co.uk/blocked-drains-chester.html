Blocked Drains in Chester

Fast, effective drain unblocking services

Professional Drain Clearance

Blocked drains cause inconvenience and potential damage if not addressed quickly. Chester Plumber Near Me provides drain unblocking services across Chester and Cheshire, using professional equipment to clear blockages effectively and restore normal drainage.

Blockages We Clear

  • Blocked sinks - Kitchen and bathroom sinks
  • Blocked toilets - Complete blockages and slow draining
  • Blocked baths and showers - Hair and soap build-up
  • Blocked external drains - Gullies and manhole blockages
  • Blocked soil pipes - Main drainage blockages

Our Methods

We use appropriate methods for each type of blockage:

  • Plunging and rodding - For simple blockages
  • High-pressure water jetting - For stubborn blockages
  • Drain inspection - To identify complex problems
  • Root cutting - For root ingress in older drains

Preventing Future Blockages

After clearing a blockage, we can advise on preventing recurrence. Common causes include grease build-up, inappropriate items flushed, and external factors like tree roots. Simple changes can often prevent repeat problems.
