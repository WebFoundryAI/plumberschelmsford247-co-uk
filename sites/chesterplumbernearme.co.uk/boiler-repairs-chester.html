Professional Boiler Repair Services

When your boiler breaks down in Chester, you need a qualified engineer who can diagnose and fix the problem quickly. Chester Plumber Near Me provides boiler repair services across Chester and Cheshire, with Gas Safe registered engineers experienced in all major boiler brands.

Whether your boiler has stopped working completely, is making strange noises, or showing error codes, we can help. Our engineers carry common spare parts to complete many repairs on the first visit.

Common Boiler Problems We Fix

  • No heating or hot water
  • Boiler not igniting or pilot light out
  • Leaking or dripping boilers
  • Low pressure problems
  • Strange noises (banging, gurgling, whistling)
  • Error codes and fault displays
  • Thermostat and control issues
  • Radiators not heating properly

Annual Boiler Servicing

Regular servicing keeps your boiler safe and efficient. Our annual service includes inspection of all components, safety checks, combustion analysis, and cleaning. This maintains warranty validity and catches potential problems early.

New Boiler Installations

When repair is no longer economical, we install new energy-efficient boilers. We advise on the right boiler for your property and complete installations to manufacturer specifications.
