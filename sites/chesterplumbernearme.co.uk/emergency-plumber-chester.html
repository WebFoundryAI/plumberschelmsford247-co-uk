What to Do in an Emergency

  1. Turn off the water - Locate your stopcock and turn clockwise
  2. Turn off the boiler - If the emergency involves heating
  3. Contain water - Use towels and containers to limit damage
  4. Switch off electrics - If water is near electrical fittings
  5. Call us - We will guide you and dispatch a plumber
